Cancellation Policy: Cancellations made prior to October 1, 2019 will receive a full refund, while those made between October 1 and October 15 (inclusive) will receive a 50% refund. No refunds are provided for any cancellations received after October 15, 2019. Modifications to registrations are permitted until October 15, 2019. Substitutions (same type) for registered attendees are permitted at any time.
